Infrastructure, Innovation and Industry

An in-depth look of the topic of the website and future targets.

Infrastructure

In the future, Infrastructure will be important because it presents an opportunity to improve trade around the world, create opportunities for new jobs, which can eventually boost economy in economy.

Better infrastructure provides efficient and sustainable transport that allows people to travel and helps avoid traffic jams

Attractive Infrastructure can help benefit an economy as it can attract tourists, which can lead to tourists spending money in the country.



Industrialization

Industrialization is the process of transforming a primarily agricultural economy to one focused on manufacturing goods. Industrialization comes with benefits for LDCs such as increased production of goods and services due to machines being used to manufacture goods efficiently.

Industrialization will continue to be a major target in the future as it provides opportunities to develop new jobs, boost economic growth and improve medical care, which overall, improves the quality of life for people.

Better Industrialization provides better health care, can help reduce the amount of greenhouse gases and creates a division of labour which helps increase output, less waste and decreased unit costs.



Innovation

Innovation is the process of creating something that is unique which ultimately stands out from the competition, like Apple

Innovation can help make a breakthrough in technology or scientific research which can boost economic growth as people would be invested in the new technology and can create the potential of new jobs to enhance innovation such as in-depth research into Quantum Computers.
